About Me

Hey there! I'm a student currently enrolled in Islington, pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computing. I've always been drawn to the world of computers ever since I got my hands on my first one. There's just something about the endless possibilities they offer that excites me.One of my biggest passions is coding. I find it incredibly rewarding to write lines of code and see them come to life as functional programs. From simple scripts to more complex applications, I enjoy the challenge of making things work and solving problems along the way. Whether it's creating a game, building a website, or developing software to automate tasks, I'm always eager to dive in and explore new coding languages and techniques.

But my love for computers doesn't stop at software. I'm equally fascinated by the hardware side of things. Learning about the components that make up a computer, how they interact with each other, and the latest advancements in technology is like uncovering the secrets of a digital playground. From processors and graphics cards to memory and storage devices, each component plays a vital role in shaping the capabilities of a computer system. Experimenting with different hardware configurations and understanding how they impact performance is something I find both intriguing and satisfying.
